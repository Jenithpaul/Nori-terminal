### Text Selection & Clipboard

Selected text is highlighted in the terminal viewport. Press <Kbd>Ctrl+C</Kbd> while a selection is active to copy it to the system clipboard (via <InlineCode>arboard</InlineCode>). Press <Kbd>Esc</Kbd> to clear the selection.

<Kbd>Ctrl+A</Kbd> with an empty input selects all visible terminal output for quick copying.
